YOU

Roses are red,
Violets are blue,
When I first met you,
A cupid's arrow struck me through,
It has a string that leads to you.

With every step, it pulls me near,
Whispering love only you can hear.
Like stars that shine in midnight’s hue,
My heart beats only just for you.

A woman sent by the almighty,
Beauty of Aphrodite,
Grace like the waves upon the sea,
Forever, you’re the one for me.
